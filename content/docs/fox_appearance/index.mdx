---
title: fox_appearance
description: Character creator, barber, and appearance persistence for the Fox RedM framework.
---

`fox_appearance` manages RedM character appearance for Fox resources.

It uses `jo_libs` for metaped components/textures, `ox_lib` callbacks/locales, `oxmysql` for persistence, and `fox_core` for the active character.
