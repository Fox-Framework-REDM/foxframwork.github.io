## Purpose

`fox_lib` is a shared helper library. It should stay small, RedM-safe, and framework-neutral where possible.

## Ownership Rules

- `ox_lib` remains responsible for callbacks, points, zones, UI, progress, and notify.
- `ox_inventory` remains responsible for items, money, weapon ownership, ammo, and metadata.
- `fox_interact` remains responsible for player-facing interaction prompts.
- `fox_lib` owns only helper lifecycle, cleanup, RedM native wrappers, and reusable UX helpers.

## Cleanup

Created entities, blips, cameras, props, and NPCs are tracked by owner resource. `fox_lib` listens to `onResourceStop` and cleans tracked objects when the owner stops.

## RedM Native Safety

Helpers use RDR3/CFX natives and avoid GTA-only concepts such as cars, plates, armor, and GTA HUD flows.
