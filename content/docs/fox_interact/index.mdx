---
title: fox_interact
description: RedM interaction system for the Fox framework.
---

`fox_interact` registers and displays world interactions for coordinates, models, entities, players, peds, animals, vehicles, and objects.

## Requirements

- `ox_lib`
- `fox_core`
- `jo_libs`
