---
title: fox_charselect
description: RedM multicharacter and spawn selector for the Fox framework.
---

`fox_charselect` replaces the default `fox_core` character selection UI with a TypeScript client/server flow and React/Vite NUI.

## Requirements

- `ox_lib`
- `oxmysql`
- `fox_core`
- `jo_libs`
