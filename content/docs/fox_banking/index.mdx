`fox_banking` is a RedM/Fox conversion of the original `ox_banking` resource by Overextended.

It keeps the account, transfer, invoice, log, and banking UI flow from the original resource while adapting access to `fox_core`, RedM bank clerks, RedM bank doors, and Fox framework resources.

## Requirements

- `fox_core`
- `fox_lib`
- `fox_interact`
- `ox_lib`
- `oxmysql`
- `ox_inventory`
- `ox_target` when `UseOxTarget` is enabled

## What It Handles

- Personal, shared, and group bank accounts.
- Money and gold deposits, withdrawals, transfers, quotes, and transaction logs.
- Branch-aware transfer fees, delays, and arrival notifications.
- Account users, ownership transfer, shared account conversion, invoices, and dashboard data.
- Bank clerk interactions through `fox_interact` or target zones through `ox_target`.
- Safe deposit vault access through `ox_inventory`.
- RedM bank door state setup from configuration.
