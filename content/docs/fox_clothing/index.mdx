---
title: fox_clothing
description: RedM clothing store and wardrobe resource for the Fox framework.
---

`fox_clothing` provides clothing stores, wardrobe outfits, wearable states, and saved clothing for RedM characters.

## Requirements

- `ox_lib`
- `oxmysql`
- `ox_inventory`
- `fox_core`
- `fox_lib`
- `jo_libs`
- `fox_interact`
