---

`fox_horses` adds server-authoritative horse and wagon gameplay for RedM on top of `fox_core`.

## Requirements

- `ox_lib`
- `oxmysql`
- `ox_inventory`
- `ox_target`
- `fox_core`
- `fox_lib`
- `fox_interact`
- `jo_libs`

## What It Handles

- Horse stables, purchase, storage, retrieval, sale, transfer, and primary horse selection.
- Active horse prompts for saddlebag, care, status, water, leading, equipment, and dismiss actions.
- Persistent status, health, loyalty, equipment, genetics, training, breeding, and saddlebag data.
- Wagon stable, wagon calls, trunk access, repair, sale, transfer, and livery support.
